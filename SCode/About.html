A little about me…

Born and raised in Guangzhou, China, I moved to the Bay Area with my family when I was a ninth grader. Growing up in two very distinct cultures taught me to be open minded and stay real to being who I am.

Graduated from San Jose State University with a BFA in Graphic Design, I am able to work across the end-to-end design process. I believe consistency and details are crucial to the success of a product. My career goal is to make impacts on people’s daily lives by providing them solutions that fulfill their needs. I’m passionate about solving problems no matter they are big or small.

Currently, I’m a UX/Product Designer at SAP designing reusable iOS components for thousands of users, also enabling product teams to build native apps efficiently. Previously, I redesigned the mobile app for a self-driving car start up—AutoX, and created brand identities at a local design agency. I’ve also had experience working with multiple clients including non-profit organizations, creating digital and print marketing collateral.

When I’m not designing…

You will find me baking spongy cakes, experimenting with new recipes, chasing my dog on the beach or decorating our home.

I’m planning to start a blog to share recipes and my life as a shiba-owner. Stay tuned!
